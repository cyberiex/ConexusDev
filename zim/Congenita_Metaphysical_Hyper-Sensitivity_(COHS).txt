====== Congenital Metaphysical Hyper-Sensitivity (CMHS) ======
Created Friday 10 May 2024

The CMHS genetic marker identifies humans with an extremely heightened sense of other realities and Gloaming Interstices. In the past these people have often been referred to as witches, warlocks, sensitives, shaman, wise-folk etc. Individuals with this marker are highly sought after by agencies dealing with cross-reality phenomena.
