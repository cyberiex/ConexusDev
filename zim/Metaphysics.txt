====== Metaphysics ======
Created Wednesday 22 May 2024

Metaphysics and paranormal science is much more widely accepted in AR02, in both the general population and  the scientific community. While here (AR00), even established and respected scientists hesitate to publish papers involving Gloaming related phenomena, in AR02, the concept is to a large extent accepted as a valid  field of investigation by most of the scientific community, leading to much more independent research and published theories.
