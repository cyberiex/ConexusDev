====== Gloaming Interstice (GI) ======
Created Friday 10 May 2024

A natural or artificially created breach in the fabric of realities, allowing Elctromagnetic radiation to pass between the realms. Although there are rumoured to be GI's large enough to allow matter to transfer, nothing of that magnitude has been verifiably detected.
